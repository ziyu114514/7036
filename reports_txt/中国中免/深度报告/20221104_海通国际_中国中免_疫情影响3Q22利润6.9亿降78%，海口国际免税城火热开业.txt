The grand opening of CDF Haikou International Duty Free City was held on October 28, 2022, with more than 800 international
and domestic famous brands, 25 brands first introduced to Hainan duty free shops and 78 exclusive CDF brands, as well as high-
end locations such as the world's largest fragrance and chemical world and Mao Tai experience shop, and cultural and tourism
feasts such as dome flying cinema and AURA Sky Forest. The number of visitors to CDFG's online shop also reached a record high,
with 2 million unique visitors, over 120,000 shoppers and sales of over RMB 500 million. According to FlightAware, the average
number of daily flights at Meilan Airport from 21 to 28 October was 197 +70% compared to the previous week, with a steady
recovery of passenger flow in Haikou. We believe that Haikou International Duty Free City will become one of the landmark
projects in the construction of Hainan's free trade port and is expected to fully undertake the strong demand from Haikou's
business travel and local consumers, contributing to the incremental business of CDFG Hainan.
The company's Haikou International Duty Free City opened successfully, and it is expected that the second phase of Sanya
International Duty Free City will be landed in 2023 and the third phase project is also on the agenda. We believe that the duty-free
consumer market in Hainan is expected to gradually pick up, and the company's share is expected to rise with the opening of new
shops. In the long term, the company will continue to expand its scale and profits and further consolidate its position as a global
leader in duty-free by grasping the opportunity of Hainan's free trade port as it cultivates its offshore duty-free business.
We expect the company's 22-24 year attributable net profit to be RMB 7.21, 13.87 and 18.14 bn respectively (original net profit
was RMB 9.22, 15.11 and 18.46 bn respectively), respectively -25.3%, +92.5% and +30.7% yoy, corresponding to EPS of RMB 3.48,
6.71 and 8.77 respectively. Considering that the company's 22-year performance was greatly affected by the COVID-19, we have
valued the 23-year performance, giving a PE of 35 times in 2023 and lowering the target price by 22% to RMB234.71 (original
target price of RMB299.99, based on 30 times EV/EBITDA in 2023), maintaining the OUTPERFORM rating.
Risks: the risk of policies, the risk of increased competition, the risk of unsuccessful expansion of cooperation with brand owners.
