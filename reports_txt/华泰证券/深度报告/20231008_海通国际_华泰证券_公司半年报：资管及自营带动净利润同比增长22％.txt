Summary
Investment Highlights:
The company applies tech-enablement, forming a competitive edge, with outstanding performance in multiple business areas.
The company is in a good operational condition with stable performances in asset management and investment banking. The
target price is RMB 18.74, and the rating "Outperform" is maintained.
Highlights: Huatai Securities released the 2023 H1 reports: Operating income reached RMB 18.37 billion, a YoY increase of 13.6%.
The net profit attributable to the parent company (NPAPC) was RMB 6.56 billion, up 22.0% YoY. The corresponding EPS record is
RMB 0.70, the ROE is 4.2%. In Q2, operating income was RMB 9.38 billion, up 5.1% YoY, and NPAPC was RMB 3.31 billion, up 4.7%
YoY. The growth in Q2 was mainly because of a 57% YoY increase in the asset management business.
Brokerage business revenue was RMB 3.15 billion, down by 11.3% YoY, and accounting for 17.2% of the operating income. The
company has a 8.08% market share of the stock base and remains the industry leader. The platform "Zhang Le Wealth" reported
average monthly active users (MAUs) of 8.85 million in H1 2023, ranking first among stock brokerage apps, with 96.24% of trading
users using this app. The balance of the company's margin trading was RMB 115.6 billion, a market share of 7.27%.
Grabbing opportunities from the deepening of capital market reforms, the company continuously advances its all-business-chain
strategy, and maintains its industry ranking. In H1 2023, the investment banking business income was RMB 1.61 billion, down
4.8% YoY. The underwriting volume for the equity business was up 19.0%; for the bond business, it increased by 6.9%. The main
underwriting volume for bonds was RMB 519.8 billion, ranking fourth.
Focusing on the total volume of asset management, the profit-making capability of public funds has increased. In H1 2023, the
asset management income was RMB 2.09 billion, up 24.9% YoY. By the end of H1 2023, the scale of Huatai Asset Management
was down by 8.9% YoY, at RMB 457 billion.
The company's transformation to a trade-oriented business model has resulted in a significant rebound in investment earnings in
Q1. In H1, net investment income (including fair value) was RMB 5.82 billion, up 55.0% YoY. In Q2, the net investment income was
RMB 1.24 billion, down 35.3% YoY.
With low valuations, the company maintains an "Outperform" rating. The EPS from 2023-2025E is expected to be RMB
1.42/1.54/1.66, and BVPS RMB 17.04/18.13/19.31. Given the company's industry-leading performance, it is given a 1.1x P/B for
2023, corresponding to a target price of RMB 18.74, with the rating maintained as "Outperform".
Risks: Market downturn leading to a decline in business scale; Equity market volatility may lead to lower than expected
investment income.
