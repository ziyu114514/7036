影响，实现了整体盈利的大幅增长。

今年上半年公司天然气与管道业务实现经营收益 136.49 亿元,较去年同期下降
63%; 剔除昆仑能源管道资产重组收益影响后, 比上年同期下降 26.5%。我们认为购

年,公司天然气销售业务板块收入同比增长27.6%,而成本同比大增了48.3%。
在盈利能力方面,今年一二季度天然气销售业务板块 EBIT 分别为 0.26 和 0.15 元
/立方米, 低于 2018-2021 年平均 0.30-0.40 元/立方米的盈利水平。

Summary
PetroChina announced its semi-annual report for 2022. In 1H22, the company realized a revenue of Rmb1,614.621bn (+34.9%)
YoY) and a NPAtS of Rmb82.388bn (equivalent to EPS of Rmb0.45) (+55.3% YoY).
The 2Q22 NPAtS is the highest in a single quarter since 2008.
Exploration and production business: still the main profit source of the company.
Refining and chemical business: Profits of oil refining and chemical business have diverged.
Sales business: The effective implementation of refined oil pricing mechanism has promoted profit growth.
Natural gas sales business: The surge in gas purchase cost restricts profitability.
• Earnings forecast and investment rating. We estimate 2022-24 EPS to be Rmb0.77/0.60/0.54 (the previous estimate:
Rmb0.60/0.55/0.53) respectively, and 2022 BPS to be Rmb7.45 (the previous estimate: Rmb7.28). With reference of valuation
of comparable companies, according to 1.0x BP in 2022, the target price is Rmb7.45 (last target price: Rmb7.28, up by 2%,
corresponding to 1.0x PB in 2022). We maintain the OUTPERFORM rating.
Risk. Prices drop of crude oil and natural gas to affect upstream business; the decline of prosperity of refining industry to affect
refining and chemical business.
