3. 
年份原浆引领消费升级，渠道精耕加速省内外增长 
徽酒龙头底蕴深厚，品牌价值再创新高。从品牌端来看，公司作为老八大名酒之
一，具备先天的品牌复苏实力。自2016年以来，公司积极赞助央视广告、高铁动车，
增加年份原浆曝光度和知名度，连续多年冠名春晚，与传统节日相结合打造“年文
化”，加深全国消费者的记忆广度。2021 年公司加入“一带一路”贸易投资论坛战略合
作伙伴，用“中国酿”贡献“世界香”，争取再造一个“数字化、国际化、法治化”的新古
井。 
聚焦消费升级，完善产品矩阵。从产品端来看，公司于2008 年推出年份原浆系
列，通过长期实践，形成了较为强大的产品培育和消费者引导能力。在省内主流价格
带升级的环境下，公司充分享受次高端扩容红利，近年来，产品增长动能顺利从古5
向古8 迁移，同时进一步借助拳头产品古20 开启了高端品牌形象之旅，完成了产品价
格带全覆盖。在收购黄鹤楼酒、明光酒业后，公司形成了以年30、古20、明绿液御
酒以及黄鹤楼大清香为产品代表的三品四香新格局。 
深化“三通工程”战略，推进全国化布局。从渠道端来看，公司作为地产酒龙头，
采用厂商1+1 模式，在掌控终端的基础上，增大费用投入，扩大销售半径，通过“路路
通、店店通、人人通”强劲的渠道拉力，实现产品陈列面最大化，推荐率最大化和消
费者指名购买率最大化，助力渠道深耕细作。在该模式下，公司能够大幅提高局部市
场占有率，并通过复制模式实现快速扩张，即“小区域、高占有”式滚动发展。古井贡
酒近年来加快全国化招商布局，以古8、古16 和古20 为重点推广对象，采取针对性
渠道模式，初步实现泛全国化布局。 
4. 
省内外竞争加剧，业绩弹性有待释放 
年份系列放量趋缓，价格贡献主要增长。2021 年年份原浆营收增速为18.8%，销
量增速为1.4%，2019 年至2021 年复合增速分别是12.5%和2.7%，年份系列销量增速
趋缓，增长主要来自价格推动。2021 年黄鹤楼营收和销量增速分别为168.7%、
100.5%，三年期间复合增速为12.4%、2.8%，受新冠疫情等不可抗因素影响，2020 年
公司与黄鹤楼就业绩承诺顺延一年的协议达成一致，2021 年黄鹤楼系列在去年低基数
下实现恢复性增长。古井贡酒系列和其他酒类三年间营收复合增速为5.8%和22.5%，
销量复合增速是15.9%和2.0%。

APPENDIX 1 
Summary 
Anhui Gujing Distillery’s brand has a profound background. It became the leader of Anhui liquor through reform and innovation. 
The company is located in Bozhou City, Anhui Province, the hometown of historical celebrities Cao Cao and Hua Tuo and one of 
the top ten spirits producing areas in the world. It has been rated as one of the top eight famous wines in China, and has won the 
gold medal in the national appraisal wine competition for four consecutive times. After the in-depth adjustment of the industry, 
the company has seized the opportunity to change its marketing model. Under the opportunity of accelerated expansion of sub-
high-end products, the company has shown a strong growth trend of both volume and price, and has been firmly in the leading 
position of Anhui spirits. 
 
The product structure continued to be optimized, and the revenue performance grew steadily. The company used the Aged 
Original Spirits to occupy the price band in advance. In 2018, the company launched the Gu 20 products and established the sub-
high-end strategy. From 2016 to 2021, the company's revenue increased from 6.02 billion yuan to 13.27 billion yuan, with a CAGR 
of 17.1% during the period, higher than other Anhui wine brands in the province. Liquor sales in 2021 increased by 16.2% year-on-
year, achieving a recovery growth before the epidemic, with a CAGR of 4.4% during the five-year period. Benefiting from the 
product structure upgrade and the slight shrinkage of the expense ratio, the net profit attributable to the parent company 
increased rapidly from 2016 to 2021, with a CAGR of 22.6% during the period. 
 
Aged Original Spirits leads the consumption upgrade, and the intensive cultivation of channels accelerates the growth inside and 
outside the province. As one of the old eight famous wines, the company has the innate brand recovery strength. It has been titled 
the Spring Festival Gala for many consecutive years, combined with traditional festivals to create "New Year's culture", and joined 
the "Belt and Road" Trade and Investment Forum as a strategic partner in 2021 to contribute to "the world's fragrance" with 
"Chinese brew". In recent years, the growth momentum of the company's products has been successfully migrated from Gu 5 to 
Gu 8, and at the same time, it has further started a high-end brand image journey with the help of the product Gu 20. After the 
acquisition of Yellow Crane Tower Wine and Mingguang Wine Industry, a new pattern of "Three Grades and Four Fragrances" has 
been formed, represented by Nian 30, Gu 20, Mingluye Royal Liquor and Yellow Crane Tower Daqingxiang. On the channel side, 
the company adopts the manufacturer 1+1 model, and presents a rolling development of "small area, high occupation" through 
the strong channel pull of the "three-way project". In recent years, the company has accelerated national investment promotion, 
focusing on the promotion of ancient 8, ancient 16 and ancient 20, and initially achieved a pan-national layout. 
 
Competition inside and outside the province has intensified, and high sales expense rates have become the norm. In 2021, the 
revenue growth rate of Aged Original Spirits was 18.8%, and the sales growth rate was 1.4%. The compound growth rate from 
2019 to 2021 was 12.5% and 2.7%, respectively. The sales growth rate of the Aged Original Spirits slowed down, and the growth 
was mainly driven by price. The company continued to raise its brand power by inputting sales expenses. The expense ratio has 
been at a high level for a long time, but its contribution to the elasticity of performance was limited. The net profit margin in 2021 
increased by 3.5pct to 17.3% compared with 2016, and decreased by 0.7pct compared with the same period last year. The 
company's revenue growth was mainly due to the support of consumption in the province, with strong geographical dependence, 
and the expansion outside the province needed to be accelerated. In 2021, the revenue share of the central China market 
dominated by Anhui, the north China market and the southern China market was 85.3%, 8.1% and 6.6% respectively, a decrease 
of 2.6pct, an increase of 2.3% and an increase of 0.2% respectively compared with 2016. 
 
We expect the company's 2022-2024 revenue to be 15.81/18.69/21.89 billion yuan, respectively, and the expected 2022-2024 EPS 
to be 5.43/6.72/8.14, respectively. With reference to the average PE of comparable companies in 2022 is 31x, the PE of Anhui 
Gujing Distillery in 2022 is 35x. The corresponding target price is 190 yuan. We initiated the company with an "neutral" rating. 
 
Risks: Industry competition intensifies; the epidemic affects demand; management capabilities are less than expected.
