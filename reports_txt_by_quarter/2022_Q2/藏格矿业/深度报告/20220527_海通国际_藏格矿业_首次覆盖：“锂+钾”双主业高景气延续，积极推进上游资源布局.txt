 国内第二大氯化钾生产企业，业绩大幅回升。公司主业以氯化
钾、碳酸锂产品为核心，辅以巨龙铜业投资经营。受益于氯化
钾、碳酸锂市场价格持续上涨以及参股巨龙铜业获得的投资收
益，公司业绩大幅提升。2022 年一季度公司实现营业总收入
12.86 亿元，同比增长199.39%；归母净利润8.14 亿元，同比增
长221.69%。 
 受益国内钾肥价格上涨。根据卓创资讯，截至2022 年5 月20
日，氯化钾（青海盐湖95%）报价4380 元/吨，同比上涨
82.50%。截止2021 年底，公司所在的察尔汗盐湖氯化钾资源保
有量约5000 万吨，年产能200 万吨，国内排名第二。根据公
告，公司2022 年计划保持100 万吨以上氯化钾产量规模，有望
显著受益氯化钾价格上涨。另外，在此基础之上公司还将依托
储备的大浪滩黑北钾等多个盐矿，于2022 年启动深层卤水勘
查，未来将根据市场判断，择时进行钾肥产量的进一步提升。 
 受益碳酸锂量价齐升。根据百川盈孚，截至2022 年5 月20 日，
电池级碳酸锂（四川99.5%min）价格为47.6 万元/吨，同比上涨
433.33%。公司目前拥有1 万吨/年电池级碳酸锂产能，有望受益
行业的高景气。同时公司通过参与产业基金间接持有西藏阿里
麻米错盐湖锂资源，锂资源量折合约218 万吨碳酸锂，根据公
告，目前该项目已基本完成立项申请报告、环价报告等约20 余
项申报资料的起草工作。此外，公司通过认购加拿大超级锂业
定向增发股份，持有该公司19.47%股份，目前该公司已启动盐
湖锂矿勘探工作。 
 参股巨龙铜业，投资收益可观。公司持有巨龙铜业30.78%股
权，巨龙铜业拥有铜储量1072 万吨，伴生钼储量57 万吨。其
中驱龙铜矿一期于2021 年12 月27 日正式投产，达产后可年产
铜约16 万吨，预计2022 年产铜12-13 万吨。同时巨龙铜业正在
进行矿山开发的总体规划，拟分阶段实施二期和三期工程，未
来若项目落地，最终可望实现年采选矿石量约2 亿吨、年产铜
超60 万吨，成为全球采选规模最大的高水平绿色矿山，将进一
步提升公司投资收益水平。 
 盈利预测与投资评级。我们预计公司2022-2024 年净利润分别为
45.83、50.28 和55.54 亿元，对应EPS 分别为2.90、3.18、3.51
元。参考可比公司估值，给予2022 年13 倍PE，对应目标价
37.70 元，首次覆盖给予“优于大市”评级。 
 风险提示。下游需求不及预期，在建项目进度不及预期，宏观
经济波动。

一、 
公司简介 
公司为国内第二大氯化钾生产企业。藏格矿业成立于1996 年，原是一家经营矿
业投资以及国际、国内贸易及珠宝的企业，2015 通过资产重组持有藏格钾肥，实现
公司主业的转型。目前公司主业以氯化钾、碳酸锂产品为核心，辅以巨龙铜业投资
经营。公司目前氯化钾年生产能力达200 万吨，为我国第二大氯化钾生产企业，近
几年实际产量均在100 万吨/年以上；拥有电池级碳酸锂产能1 万吨/年。 
截至2022 年一季度，公司前三大股东分别为西藏藏格创业投资集团、宁波梅山
保税港区新沙鸿运投资、永鸿实业，分别持有公司28.38%、18.17%、11.02%。公司
实控人为肖永明，通过直接或间接方式共计持有公司42.15%股份。

主营业务分析

增长523.60%。2022 年一季度公司实现营收12.86 亿元，同比增长199.39%；归母净
利润8.14 亿元，同比增长221.69%。

APPENDIX 1 
Summary 
 The second largest potassium chloride production enterprise in China, the performance rebounded sharply. The main 
business of the company is potassium chloride and lithium carbonate products as the core, supplemented by the investment 
and operation of Julong Copper. Benefiting from the continuous rise in the market prices of potassium chloride and lithium 
carbonate, as well as the investment income obtained from the shareholding of Julong Copper, the company's performance has 
improved significantly. In the first quarter of 2022, the company achieved a total operating income of Rmb1.286bn, a year-on-
year increase of 199.39%; net profit attributable to the parent was Rmb814mn, a year-on-year increase of 221.69%. 
 Benefit from rising domestic potash fertilizer prices. According to Zhuochuang Information, as of May 20, 2022, the quotation 
of potassium chloride (95% of Qinghai Salt Lake) was Rmb4380 / ton, an increase of 82.50% year-on-year. As of the end of 2021, 
the Qarhan Salt Lake where the company is located has about 50 million tons of potassium chloride resources, with an annual 
production capacity of 2 million tons, ranking second in China. According to the announcement, the company plans to maintain 
a production scale of more than 1 million tons of potassium chloride in 2022, which is expected to significantly benefit from the 
rise in potassium chloride prices. In addition, on this basis, the company will also rely on the reserves of Dalangtan Heibei 
Potassium and other salt mines to start deep brine exploration in 2022. In the future, according to market judgments, the 
production of potash fertilizer will be further increased at a certain time. 
 Benefit from the rise in volume and price of lithium carbonate. According to Baichuan Yingfu, as of May 20, 2022, the price of 
battery-grade lithium carbonate (Sichuan 99.5% min) was Rmb476000 /ton, a year-on-year increase of 433.33%. The company 
currently has a capacity of 10,000 tons/year of battery-grade lithium carbonate, which is expected to benefit from the high 
prosperity of the industry. At the same time, the company indirectly holds the lithium resources of Tibet Ali Ma Mi Co salt lake 
through participating in the industrial fund. The lithium resources are equivalent to about 2.18 million tons of lithium carbonate. 
According to the announcement, the project has basically completed about 20 projects such as the project application report 
and the ring price report. Drafting of application materials. In addition, the company held 19.47% of the company's shares by 
subscribing for the additional shares of Canadian Super Lithium Industry. At present, the company has started the exploration 
of salt lake lithium mine. 
 Participating in the giant dragon copper industry, the investment income is considerable. The company holds a 30.78% stake 
in Julong Copper, which has 10.72 million tons of copper reserves and 570,000 tons of associated molybdenum reserves. Among 
them, the first phase of Qulong Copper Mine was officially put into production on December 27, 2021. After reaching production, 
it can produce about 160,000 tons of copper annually. It is expected to produce 120,000-130,000 tons of copper in 2022. At the 
same time, Julong Copper is carrying out the overall planning of mine development, and plans to implement the second and 
third phases of the project in stages. If the project is implemented in the future, it is expected that the annual mining and 
processing ore volume will be about 200 million tons and the annual copper output will exceed 600,000 tons. It has become the 
world's largest high-level green mine with the largest mining and selection scale, which will further enhance the company's 
investment income level. 
 Earnings forecast and investment rating. We estimate that the company's NPAtS in 2022-2024 will be Rmb4.583bn, 
Rmb5.028bn, and Rmb5.554bn respectively, with corresponding EPS of Rmb2.90, 3.18, Rmb3.51. With the valuation of 
comparable companies, we value the company at FY22E PER of 13x with a target price of Rmb37.70. We initiate with a 
"OUTPERFORM" rating. 
 Risk. The downstream demand is lower than expected, the progress of projects under construction is lower than expected, and 
the macro economy fluctuates.
