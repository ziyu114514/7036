 事件：立讯精密2021 年实现营业收入1539.46 亿元（+66.43%）
（括号表示年同比，下同），归母净利润70.71 亿元 
（-2.14%），扣非后归母净利润60.16 亿元（-1.2%）。1Q22 年
实现营业收入416.00 亿元（+97.91%），归母净利润18.03 亿元
（+33.63%），扣非后归母净利润15.24 亿元（+30.56%）。 
 财务维度：1Q22 盈利环比改善，21 年各项业务同比均大幅增
长。落实“三个五年”战略部署，充分发挥各产品线/业务模块间
的高效协同，2021 年各项业务均实现较大幅度的收入增长，电
脑互联产品及精密组件/汽车互联产品及精密组件/通讯互联产
品及精密组件/ 消费性电子的收入分别达到78.6/41.4/ 
32.7/1346.4 亿元，同比分别+123.1%/+45.7%/+44.3%/+64.6%。
1Q22归母净利润/扣非归母净利润增速环比大幅改善、毛利率及
净利率双升。 
 消费电子：行业增长带动一站式解决方案提供商成长。根据21
年报援引Statista 统计，2015 年至2020 年全球消费电子行业市
场规模呈现出逐步扩大的趋势，5 年间全球消费电子市场规模的
年复合增长率达到2.90%；预计到2025 年，全球消费电子市场
规模将达到10980 亿美元。在5G 与AI 技术进步的持续推动下，
智能移动、智能可穿戴、AR/VR/MR、智能家居及智能显示等
IoT 设备的发展将百花齐放，随着终端产品更新换代速度的逐渐
加快，内部电子元件的集成化程度亦相应增长，技术门槛不断
提高，也进而带动零部件及成品的机会。公司稳步有序丰富产
品线类别，包括声学产品TWS 蓝牙耳机等，智能生活类5G 
CPE 、Wifi 可携式路由，智能终端类可穿戴手表手环、
AR/VR/MR，计算机配件类Dock 扩充坞，智能触控笔、视频会议
设备及各类零售PC/NB/手机品牌的有线与无线高瓦数快充配件
等。 
 通信与数据中心：高速增长期。随着互联网的发展不断兴起，
电商、视频、云计算等互联网垂直行业快速崛起，全球数据中
心市场迎来一轮又一轮高速增长期，根据21 年报援引IDC 数据
显示，2021 年全球服务器市场出货量和销售额分别为1353.9 万
台和992.2 亿美元，同比增长6.9%和6.4%；2012-2021 年，全球
互联网数据中心行业市场规模的年均复合增速超过10%。IDC 预
计到2027 年，全球服务器市场规模将达到1265 亿美元。2022
年，公司将进行服务器等整机组装业务的布局，围绕整机加强
核心零部件的双线发展战略，逐步成为数据与通信的多品类综
合解决方案供应商。

 汽车电子：从政策驱动过渡到市场驱动。随着“智能化”的普及，汽车将变成大型
移动智能与计算终端，下一阶段主机厂将需要通过建立生态体系来突出重围，将
汽车和消费电子进行关联，构建更多消费场景，实现“人-车-家”全场景覆盖。根
据21 年报援引Markets and Markets 数据，2021 年全球汽车线束市场规模预计达
到470 亿美元。公司针对汽车板块进行长期性规划，产品布局为线束，连接系
统，新能源，智能网联，智能驾舱以及智能制造等。 
 组建合资公司，进军ODM 整车研发，深度发展Tier1。2022 年2 月，公司与奇瑞
集团签署《战略合作框架协议》，公司拟与奇瑞新能源汽车股份有限公司共同组
建合资公司，专业从事新能源汽车的ODM 整车研发及制造，通过该为品牌客户
造车的业务平台，为立讯精密汽车核心零部件业务提供前沿的研发设计、量产平
台及出海口，深度发展公司Tier1 业务。 
 盈利预测及投资评级。公司坚定长期主义，加上三个主要业务收益于所在行业宏
观增长，未来收入将会有所提升，但营业支出同时相应新业务发展而增加。我们
调增2022-23 年收入预测各24%，调降净利润预测17% 和12%，并引入2024 年预
测；预计立讯精密2022 年至2024 年EPS 分别为1.44、1.97、2.40 元。结合可比
公司估值情况，给予立讯精密2022 年28 倍PE 估值（此前为 2021 年 45 倍 PE），
对应目标价为40.32 元（此前为 58.5 元），给予“优于大市”评级。 
 风险提示：新能源汽车市场发展进程缓慢或不及预期的风险；5G 建设进度变缓或
政策变化带来的风险；受全球宏观经济下行的影响，消费电子市场需求不及预
期。

APPENDIX 1 
Summary 
 
Event. Luxshare Precision reported 2021 revenue of 153.95 billion yuan (+66% YoY), net profit attributable to the parent of 
7.071 billion yuan (-2.14% YoY), and net profit of 6.016 billion yuan after deduction (-1.2% YoY). In 1Q22, the operating income 
was 41.600 billion yuan (+97.91% YoY), the net profit attributable to the parent was 1.803 billion yuan (+33.63% YoY), and 
the net profit attributable to the parent after deduction was 1.524 billion yuan (+30.56% YoY). 
 
 
Financials. Profitability in 1Q22 improved QoQ, and all businesses in 21 years saw significant year-on-year growth. Thanks to 
the synergy between different product lines, all business segments have shown significant revenue growth in 2021.  
 
 
Consumer Electronics: Industry growth drives the growth of one-stop solution providers. According to Statista, the market 
size of the global consumer electronics industry has shown a trend of gradual expansion from 2015 to 2020, with a CAGR of 
2.90% in the five years. 
 
 
Telecom and data center: high growth period. With the continuous development of the Internet, the rapid rise of Internet 
industries such as e-commerce, video, and cloud computing, the global data center market has ushered in a period of rapid 
growth. According to IDC data cited in the 21 annual report, the global server market in 2021 Shipments and sales were 
13.539 million units and US$99.22 billion, up 6.9% and 6.4% year-on-year. IDC predicts that by 2027, the global server market 
will reach $126.5 billion. In 2022, the company will adopt the strategy to develop assembly business to become a multi-
category comprehensive solution provider of data and communication. 
 
 
Automotive Electronics: From Policy-Driven to Market-Driven. With the popularization of "intelligence", automobiles will 
become large-scale mobile intelligence and computing terminals. In the next stage, OEMs will need to establish an ecosystem 
to stand out, such as linking automobiles and consumer electronics, building more consumption scenarios, and achieving 
"People-Car-Home" full coverage. According to Markets and Markets, the global automotive wiring harness market is 
expected to reach US$47 billion in 2021. The company carries out long-term planning for the automotive sector, and the 
product offerings include wiring harness, connection system, new energy, intelligent network connection, intelligent cockpit 
and intelligent manufacturing. 
 
 
Enter the ODM vehicle business through joint venture. In February 2022, the company announced collaboration with Chery 
Group. The company plans to form a joint venture company with Chery New Energy Vehicle Co., Ltd., specializing in electric 
vehicles. The ODM focuses on R&D and manufacturing automobiles. Through this business platform, Luxshare builds vehicles 
for brand customers, acquires cutting-edge R&D design, mass production platform and overseas export channel to develops 
the company's Tier1 business. 
 
 
Earning forecast and rating.  The company is long-term focused, coupled with the industry growth trend of its three major 
business segments. We expect the OPEX growth may outpace the top line in the short term. As a result, we enhance our 
2022-23 revenue estimate by 24% but cut the net earnings by 17% and 12%. The 2022-24 EPS are expected to reach Rmb1.44, 
Rmb1.97, and Rmb2.40 (newly introduced) respectively. Considering the valuations of comparable companies, we cut our 
target price to Rmb40.32 on 28x of 2022 PER from the previous of Rmb58.5 on 45x PER of 2021. The OUTPERFORM rating 
remains. 
 
 
Risks: 1) Slower than expected development of EV market 2) Slower than expected 5G development; 3) Lower than expected 
consumer electronics demand
