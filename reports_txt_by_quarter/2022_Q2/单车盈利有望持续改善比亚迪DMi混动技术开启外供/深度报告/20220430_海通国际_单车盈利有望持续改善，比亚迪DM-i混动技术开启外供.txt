事件 
比亚迪发布一季报，1Q22 实现营业收入668.3 亿元，同比增长
63.0%；实现归母净利润8.1 亿元，同比增长240.6%，环比增长
34.3%，扣非后净利润5.1 亿元，实现同比扭亏为盈。 
点评 
一季度业绩亮眼，在手订单逐月增加。公司一季度汽车&电池等
主营业务（不包括电子）实现营收458.9亿元，同比增长117.5%，
得益于一季度公司新能源汽车销量再创新高，市场份额迅猛增
长。1Q22汽车&电池等主营业务毛利71.4亿元，同比增长84.0%；
毛利率15.6%，较4Q21 环比下滑0.9 个百分点，在上游关键原材
料持续涨价背景下，业绩表现亮眼。公司一季度累计销量28.6 万
辆新能源车，同比增423%；其中新能源乘用车累计销量28.5 万
辆，同比大幅增长433%，市场份额达到24%。公司自2022 年3 月
起停产燃油车，专注于EV+PHEV 新能源汽车的研发和销售，目前
累计在手订单量达40 万辆，并且随着产品矩阵的不断丰富，未交
付订单仍在逐月增加。 
单车盈利有望持续改善。我们估算公司1Q22 单车净利润约2368
元，较4Q21 环比增长约623 元/26%。公司今年于2 月1 日和3 月
16 日两度上调车型价格，我们考虑到公司目前在手订单较多，涨
价对盈利的正面影响有望从二季度起逐渐体现。公司4 月发布新
款汉家族系列产品，汉 DM-i 全面对标燃油车，有望在二线及以下
新能源车市场加速渗透，带动中国混合动力市场迎来爆发；同
时，多款基于e3.0 平台的海洋系列新车即将上市，我们认为单车
盈利有望继续改善。 
创维新车发布开启比亚迪DM-i 外供时代。根据OFweek 消息，创
维汽车于4 月27 日发布混动SUV 创维HT-i，预售价14.98-19.98 万
元。动力方面，创维HT-i 将首度搭载比亚迪DM-i 混动系统，其中
包括比亚迪自研的骁云-插混1.5L 高效发动机及EHS 电混系统动力
总成。搭载33kWh 超混专用电池，纯电续航里程达到200km，综
合续航里程为1267km，百公里亏电油耗低至5.3L。此前比亚迪已
通过弗迪电池开启刀片电池的外供，并将e 平台 3.0 向行业开放共
享，我们认为本次创维新车发布正式拉开了DM-i 技术外供的大
幕，将进一步巩固比亚迪混动技术的市场影响力和领先优势。 
盈利预测与投资评级：我们对公司保持长期看好。由于公司全面
转型新能源，给予2022-24 EPS 每股2.53/4.41/6.54 元（基本持
平）。我们采用SOTP 估值法，参照可比公司估值，分别给予2022
年汽车/手机/电池/半导体分部6,687/236/685/325 亿元市值估值，
加总得出合理市值7,933 亿元，对应合理目标价272.5 元（下调
4%），维持“优于大市”评级。 
风险提示：动力电池等原材料涨价风险，新业务拓展进度不及预
期，新能源汽车行业景气度不及预期。

APPENDIX 1 
Summary 
Events 
BYD released its first-quarter report. In 1Q22, its revenue was Rmb66.83bn, +63.0% YoY; net profit attributable to shareholders 
(NPAtS) was Rmb810mn, +240.6% YoY/+34.3% QoQ. NPAtS after deduction of non-recurrent G/L was Rmb510mn, turning losses 
into profits YoY. 
 
Comments 
The Q1 performance was outstanding, and the orders in hand increased month by month. In the first quarter, the company's 
main businesses including automobiles & batteries (excluding electronics) achieved revenue of Rmb45.89bn, +117.5% YoY, 
benefiting from the fact that the company's NEV sales hitting a new high in the first quarter, and its market share grew rapidly. In 
1Q22, the gross profit of main businesses was Rmb7.14bn, +84.0% YoY. The gross profit margin was 15.6%, -0.9pcts QoQ compared 
with 4Q21. The performance was outstanding against the background of continuous price hikes of upstream key raw materials. In 
1Q22, the company sold over 286k NEVs, +423% YoY, among which the cumulative sales of new energy passenger vehicles was 
285k vehicles, a significant increase of 433% YoY, and the market share reached 24%. The company has stopped production of ICEs 
since March 2022, focusing on the R&D and sales of EV+PHEVs. Currently, the cumulative number of orders in hand has reached 
400,000 vehicles, and with the continuous enrichment of the product matrix, un-delivered orders are still increasing month by 
month. 
 
Per-vehicle profitability is expected to continue improving. We estimate that the company's 1Q22 per-vehicle net profit is about 
Rmb2,368, +Rmb623/26% QoQ compared with 4Q21. The company raised model prices twice this year on February 1 and March 
16. Considering that the company currently has more orders on hand, the positive impact of price increases on earnings is expected 
to be gradually reflected from 2Q22. The company released a new series of Han family products in April. The Han DM-i fully 
benchmarks ICEs to accelerate penetration in the second-tier and below NEV market, driving the explosion of China's hybrid 
market. At the same time, a number of new Ocean series models based on the e3.0 platform will be launched soon, and we believe 
that the per-vehicle profitability is expected to continue improving. 
 
The launch of Skyworth's new model opens the era of BYD DM-i external supply. According to OFweek news, Skyworth 
Automobile released the hybrid SUV Skyworth HT-i on April 27, with a pre-sale price of Rmb149,800-199,800. In terms of power, 
Skyworth HT-i will be equipped with BYD DM-i hybrid system for the first time, including BYD's self-developed Xiaoyun PHEV 1.5L 
high-efficiency engine and EHS electric hybrid system powertrain. Equipped with a 33kWh super-hybrid special battery, the pure 
electric cruising range reaches 200km, the comprehensive cruising range is 1267km, and the fuel consumption per 100 kilometers 
is as low as 5.3L. Previously, BYD has opened the external supply of blade batteries through FinDreams Battery, and has opened 
the e-platform 3.0 to the industry for sharing. We believe that the launch of the new Skyworth new model officially opened the 
era of the external supply of DM-i technology, which will further consolidate the market influence and leading edge of BYD's hybrid 
technology. 
 
Earnings forecast and investment rating: We maintain a long-term bullish view on the company. As the company comprehensively 
transforms into NEV automakers, we roughly raise the company's EPS expectation in 2022-24 to be Rmb2.53, 4.41, 6.54 per share, 
respectively. We apply the SOTP valuation method and refer to the valuation of comparable companies, value BYD’s 
automobile/mobile phone/battery/semiconductor segments at market value of Rmb668.7bn/23.6bn/68.5bn/32.5bn in 2022, and 
add up to a total market value of Rmb793.3bn, with target price of Rmb272.5 (cut 4%). We maintain with "OUTPERFORM" rating. 
 
Risks: The risk of rising prices of raw materials such as power batteries; the progress of new business development is less than 
expected; the NEV industry development is not as expected.
