AIDC 业务结构优化，盈利能力正在边际改善 
润泽科技(300442)
资本货物/工业 
 公司跟踪报告 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
本报告导读： 
中报业绩稳健； AIDC 业务结构优化；一线核心资源限制严格，更具稀缺性；REITs 成
功上市，“轻重分离”模式打通。 
投资要点： 
 
 维持“优于大市”评级。我们预计，公司2025-2027 年营业收入分别
为62.82/76.36/91.09 亿元（原2025-26 预测为77.31/99.67 亿元），同
比增长43.93%/21.55%/19.29%；归母净利润分别为26.94/33.36/40.50
亿元（原2025-26 预测为30.83/40.49 亿元），同比增长50.49%/23.82%
/21.42%；EPS 分别为1.65/2.04/2.48 元。参考可比公司，给予公司
2025 年动态45 倍PE（原为2024 年32x），目标价74.18 元（+79%），
维持“优于大市”评级。 
 
中报业绩稳健。2025 年上半年，公司营收24.96 亿元，同比增长
15.31%，归母净利润8.82 亿元，同比下降8.73%。利润短期承压主
因是上半年大规模交付约220MW，折旧、电费等成本先行，而收入
确认有爬坡周期。公司披露归母净利季度环比增长5.06%，说明边
际正在改善。 
 
AIDC 业务结构优化。2024 年，公司引流相关业务以一次性收入为为
主。2025 上半年，AIDC 业务重心已全面转向智算服务，且上半年智
算服务收入占比已经较高，AIDC 业务结构实现显著优化，其业务稳
定性与盈利持续性均大幅提升。 
 
一线核心资源限制严格，更具稀缺性。2025 年以来，一线城市及其
周边的土地和能耗限制更加严格，因此，非一线城市订单开始起量。
同时，随着机柜功率的指数级跳升，电力资源肯定也是越来越紧张，
所以一线城市的优质机柜资源更具有稀缺性。 
 
REITs 成功上市，“轻重分离”模式打通。公司南方润泽科技数据中心
REIT 成功上市。REITs 平台的搭建盘活了存量优质资产，优化了资本
结构，打通了“重资产基础设施+轻资产运维服务”的业务模式。我们
认为，公司公募REITs 的成功发行，为公司提供了坚实的资金和工具
支持，将进一步巩固其市场地位。 
 
风险提示。AIDC 的需求增长不及预期；IDC 业务上架率不及预期影
响净利润；市场竞争加剧。

APPENDIX 1 
Summary 
Investment Highlights: 
Maintain ‘Outperform’ rating. We project the company’s 2025-2027 revenue at 6.28/7.64/9.11 billion RMB, growing 
43.93%/21.55%/19.29% YoY; net profit attributable to shareholders at 2.69/3.34/4.05 billion RMB, growing 
50.49%/23.82%/21.42% YoY; EPS at 1.65/2.04/2.48 RMB. Based on comparable companies, assign a 45x PE for 2025, target 
price 74.18 RMB, maintain ‘Outperform’ rating. 
Interim report shows stable performance. In H1 2025, revenue was 2.50 billion RMB, up 15.31% YoY, net profit attributable to 
shareholders was 0.88 billion RMB, down 8.73% YoY. Short-term profit pressure due to large-scale delivery of about 220MW, 
with costs like depreciation and electricity preceding revenue recognition. Quarterly NPAtS grew 5.06%, indicating marginal 
improvement. 
AIDC business structure optimized. In 2024, the focus was on one-time revenue. By H1 2025, AIDC shifted to intelligent 
computing services, with a high revenue share, significantly optimizing structure, enhancing stability and profitability. 
Strict core resource limits in top-tier cities increase scarcity. Since 2025, stricter land and energy consumption limits in top-tier 
cities have led to increased orders in non-top-tier cities. With exponential growth in cabinet power, electricity resources are 
increasingly scarce, making top-tier city cabinet resources more valuable. 
Successful REITs listing, enabling ‘asset-light and heavy’ model. The successful listing of the Range Intelligent Computing 
Technology Group Company Limited’s REIT activated quality assets, optimized capital structure, and enabled the ‘heavy asset 
infrastructure + light asset operation’ model. The successful public REITs issuance provides solid financial and tool support, 
further solidifying market position. 
Risk Warning: AIDC demand growth weaker than expected; IDC business rack rate weaker than expected affecting net profit; 
intensified market competition.
