事件：公司发布三季报，3Q23 实现收入61.6 亿 (+35% QoQ/+35% 
YoY)，净利润10.8 亿 (-10% QoQ/+16% YoY)，扣非净利润10.3 亿 
（-4% QoQ/+24% YoY）。公司截至三季度合同负债93.8 亿元，较
二季度末85.9 亿元持续增长。同时，公司披露前三季度电子工艺
装备收入129 亿元，同比增长63%。 
BIS 制裁影响有限，光刻机非扩产瓶颈：美国BIS 于17 日晚发
布新版限制法案，新增对于若干符合特定参数的光刻、沉积、刻
蚀、外延等设备的管制。市场比较关心的光刻设备方面，虽然BIS
将ASML 所有在售Immersion DUV 划入管治范围，但是ASML 表示
对NXT：1980Di 系列的管制仅影响若干具备先进制程制造能力的晶
圆厂（针对特定工厂而非公司），对其余客户出货无需获得美方
许可。另一方面， ASML 获得荷兰政府批准，可以持续向中国客户
提供先进DUV（NXT：2000 及以上机型）直至年底，同时三季度中
国区收入24.4 亿欧元（占比46%），且四季度收入占比将继续上
升。我们认为国内晶圆厂将获得充足的光刻机供给，在中期维度
内，光刻设备并非先进制程以及成熟制程扩产的瓶颈。 
周期见底，24 年国内WFE 持续增长：目前半导体周期已经出
现复苏迹象， HPC 和智能手机等终端产品需求逐步回暖， 同时晶
圆厂逻辑制程产能利用率也已经开始回升，下游库存回到正常水
平。在周期触底反弹的情况下，我们认为晶圆厂扩产进度将会有
所加速。 另一方面，LAM Research 三季度业绩会上调全球WFE 至 
$80bn 以上（前次预期为$75bn），主要来自中国受限晶圆厂支
出。相应的，我们现在预期中国WFE 市场2023/2024 年将均保持
增长。我们建议后续关注重点项目扩产情况，包括国内头部晶圆
厂北京扩产项目以及长存二期等。 
估值与建议：目前公司估值具备较强吸引力，公司同业中微公司
/盛美上海/拓荆科技分别对应55x/51x/60x 2024PE，其2023-2025年
利润复合增速分别为
19%/27%/46% 。我们预计公司
2023/2024/2025 年净利润分别为34.7/54.7/66.4 亿元，2023-2025
年利润复合增速38%，目标价362 元对应35x 2024PE（历史平均远
期PE，估值不变），维持优于大市评级。 
风险：1）新产品推广不及预期；2）竞争加剧；3）铜价持续上涨
冲击盈利能力；4）产能释放不及预期。

APPENDIX 1 
Summary 
Event: The company released its third quarterly report, with revenue of 6.16 billion (+35% QoQ/+35% YoY), net profit of 1.08 
billion (-10% QoQ/+16% YoY) and ex non-ordinary profit of 1.03 billion (-4% QoQ/+24% YoY) in 3Q23. As of the third quarter, the 
company's contract liabilities were 9.38 billion yuan, a continuous increase from 8.59 billion yuan at the end of the second quarter. 
At the same time, the company disclosed that the revenue of electronic process equipment in the first three quarters was 12.9 
billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 63%. 
 
The impact of BIS sanctions is limited, and lithography machines are not bottlenecks for expansion: BIS in the United States 
issued a new version of the restriction bill on the evening of the 17th, adding new controls on a number of lithography, deposition, 
etching, epitaxy and other equipment that meet specific parameters. In terms of lithography equipment, which the market is more 
concerned about, although BIS has included all of ASML's Immersion DUVs on sale, ASML said that the regulation of the NXT:1980Di 
series will only affect a few fabs with advanced process manufacturing capabilities (for specific fabs and not companies), and the 
rest of the customers do not need to obtain a U.S. license for shipments. On the other hand, ASML has received approval from the 
Dutch government to continue to provide advanced DUVs (NXT: 2000 and above) to Chinese customers until the end of the year, 
while revenue in China in the third quarter was 2.44 billion euros (46%), and the proportion of revenue in the fourth quarter will 
continue to rise. We believe that domestic fabs will receive sufficient supply of lithography machines, and in the medium term, 
lithography equipment is not a bottleneck for the expansion of advanced processes and mature processes. 
 
The cycle bottomed out, and the domestic WFE continued to grow in 24 years: At present, the semiconductor cycle has shown 
signs of recovery, and the demand for end products such as HPC and smartphones has gradually recovered, while the capacity 
utilization rate of the logic process of the wafer factory has also begun to rise, and the downstream inventory has returned to 
normal levels. In the case of a bottoming out cycle, we believe that the fab expansion schedule will accelerate. On the other hand, 
LAM Research's Q3 results were revised upwards to more than $80bn (previous forecast of $75bn), mainly due to restricted fab 
spending in China. Correspondingly, we now expect the Chinese WFE market to grow in 2023/2024. We recommend paying 
attention to the expansion of key projects in the future, including Top domestic foundry Beijing Project and Changcun Phase II. 
 
Valuation and recommendation: At present, the company's valuation is highly attractive, and the company's peers in the industry, 
AMEC/ACM Shanghai/Piotech, correspond to 55x/51x/60x 2024PE respectively, and its profit compound growth rate from 2023 
to 2025 is 19%/27%/46% respectively. We expect the company's net profit in 2023/2024/2025 to be 34.7/54.7/6.64 billion yuan 
respectively, with a compound profit growth rate of 38% from 2023 to 2025, and a target price of 362 yuan corresponding to 35x 
2024PE (historical average forward PE, unchanged valuation), maintaining an outperform rating. 
 
Risks: 1) Delayed launch of new products; 2) Competition intensified; 3) Increasing copper prices hitting profitability; 4) Slower 
capacity expansion.
