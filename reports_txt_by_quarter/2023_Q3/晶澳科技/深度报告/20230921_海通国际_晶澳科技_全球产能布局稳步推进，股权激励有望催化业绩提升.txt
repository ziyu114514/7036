公司发布2023 年中报，盈利能力显著提升。2023H1 公司实现营业收入408.43

实现归母净利润 22.31 亿元，同比增长 134.36%，环比下降 13.59%。 
海外市场持续发力，组件出货量利齐升。公司2023H1 组件出货量为23.95GW
（含自用497MW），其中约55%来自海外市场，约34%来自分销渠道。2023Q2
的出货量约为12.4GW，同比增长41%，环比增长7.5%，预计预计全年出货量
将达到60-65GW，同比增长50%以上。上半年硅料、硅片价格大幅下滑，推动
公司毛利水平显著改善，公司2023H1 毛利率同比提升6.6pct，达到19.2%，其
中国内市场毛利率同比增加 2.70 pct， 提升至 16.12%，海外市场毛利率同比增
加 8.52  pct，提升至 21.63%。 
全球领先的垂直一体化光伏组件制造商，各环节产能规模有序增加。
2023H1，公司顺利投产了越南2.5GW 拉晶及切片、包头10GW 拉晶及切片、
宁晋 6GW 电池片、邢台 10GW 组件、合肥10GW 组件项目。同时，包头、宁
晋、扬州等项目总计 60GW 拉晶及切片、80GW 电池及 32 GW 组件产能正稳步
推进。此外，在海外市场的产能投资，公司在东南亚已经建立了4GW 的一体
化产能，美国2GW组件项目正按计划建设，以及拟在越南投资5GW的TOPCon
电池片产能；在欧美贸易政策的限制下，公司有望通过在海外建厂收获更高
溢价的订单。到2023 年底，公司全球的组件产能将达到95GW，硅片和电池
片的产能约为组件的90%，自给率的提升有望进一步协同降本；其中N 型电池
片产能将超过57GW，预计2023 年出货约 20GW，占比将达到 30%。 
持续加大研发投入，致力于长期提质增效。公司2023H1 研发投入为22.28 亿
元，同比增长8.78%，占营收约5.46%。公司积极顺应N 型技术迭代，不断提
升产品技术工艺。电池方面，公司最新推出的N 型倍秀（Bycium+），量产最高
转换效率可达25.6%。组件方面，公司发布了全新 N 型组件产品 Deep Blue 4.0 
Pro，采用了公司自研的高寿命低含氧量N 型182mm*199mm 硅片，叠加 
Bycium+电池技术，并集成了 SMBB、高密度封装等提质增效技术，量产效率
达到22.5%，72 版型最高功率可达 630W；相较于P 型组件，具备更低的BOS
成本以及LCOE 优势。 
制定考核目标，实施股权激励。公司拟向2733 名对象授予340 万股限制性股
票和8021 万份股票期权，业绩考核目标为2023-2025 年营业收入达到
1000/1200/1500 亿元或净利润达到95/125/160 亿元，将为业绩增长提供信
心。 
盈利预测与投资建议：基于公司上半年业绩增长亮眼以及股权激励机制完
善，我们上调公司2023-2025 年盈利预测。我们预计公司2023-2025 年归母净
利润分别为94.99/121.95/155.95 亿元，摊薄EPS 分别为2.87/3.68/4.71 元。公
司作为领先N型一体化产能领先企业，预计随着海内外产能逐步释放，公司业
务能力将持续增强，同时相较于可比公司享受一定的估值溢价，但考虑到当
前板块估值中枢下移，以及考虑到公司在4 月末进行1.4 比1 股本转增，我们
给于公司2023 年18 倍PE（原先为24 倍），下调目标价至51.66 元（原先为
61.43 元），维持 “优于大市” 评级。 
风险提示：产能投产不及预期、行业竞争加剧、原材料价格波动等。

APENDIX 1 
Summary 
In the 2023 interim report, the company has announced a significant improvement in its profitability. In 2023 H1, the company achieved 
a total revenue of RMB 40.843 billion, representing a year-on-year increase of 43.47%. The net profit attributable to shareholders reached 
RMB 4.843 billion, showing a year-on-year growth of 182.85%. In 2023 Q2, the company generated 啊total revenue of RMB 20.362 
billion, with a year-on-year growth of 26.10% but a sequential decrease of 0.58%. The net profit attributable to shareholders in Q2 was 
RMB 2.231 billion, a yoy increase of 134.36% but a qoq decrease of 13.59%. 
 
The company's overseas market has continued to strengthen, leading to a remarkable increase in component shipments. In H1 2023, the 
company shipped a total of 23.95 GW of components (including 497 MW for self-use), with approximately 55% coming from overseas 
markets and approximately 34% from distribution channels. The shipment volume in Q2 2023 was approximately 12.4 GW, a yoy increase 
of 41% and a qoq increase of 7.5%. It is projected that the total annual shipment volume will reach 60-65 GW, representing a yoq increase 
of over 50%. The substantial decline in the prices of silicon materials and wafers in the first half of the year significantly improved the 
company's gross profit level. The gross profit margin in H1 2023 increased by 6.6 pct year-on-year, reaching 19.2%. Specifically, the GPM 
in the domestic market increased by 2.70 pct year-on-year to 16.12%, while the GPM in the overseas market increased by 8.52 pct year-
on-year to 21.63%. 
 
As a globally leading vertically integrated PV module manufacturer, the company has been steadily increasing its capacity across various 
stages. In H1 2023, the company successfully commenced production at the 2.5 GW ingot and wafer facility in Vietnam, the 10 GW ingot 
and wafer facility in Baotou, the 6 GW cell facility in Ningjin, the 10 GW module facility in Xingtai, and the 10 GW module facility in Hefei. 
Simultaneously, the total capacity of ingot and wafer facilities in Baotou, Ningjin, Yangzhou, and other projects is steadily progressing 
towards 60 GW, with 80 GW for cells and 32 GW for modules. Furthermore, in terms of overseas market capacity investments, the 
company has established a 4 GW integrated capacity in Southeast Asia, and the construction of a 2 GW module project in the United 
States is proceeding as planned. Additionally, there are plans to invest in a 5 GW TOPCon cell capacity in Vietnam. Under the restrictions 
of trade policies in Europe and the United States, the company is expected to gain higher premium orders by establishing overseas 
factories. By the end of 2023, the company's global module capacity will reach 95 GW, with the capacity of wafers and cells amounting 
to approximately 90% of the module capacity. The increased self-sufficiency rate is expected to further reduce costs. The capacity of N-
type cells will exceed 57 GW, and the estimated shipment for 2023 will be approximately 20 GW, accounting for 30% of the total 
shipments. 
 
The company continues to increase its R&D investment, aiming for long-term quality improvement and efficiency enhancement. The R&D 
investment in H1 2023 amounted to RMB 2.228 billion, showing a yoy increase of 8.78%, accounting for approximately 5.46% of the 
revenue. The company actively embraces N-type technology iterations and continuously enhances its product technology and processes. 
For cells, the company has introduced the latest N-type product called "Bycium+" with a mass production conversion efficiency of up to 
25.6%. As for modules, the company has launched the new N-type module product "Deep Blue 4.0 Pro," which utilizes the company's 
self-developed low-oxygen N-type 182mm*199mm wafers, integrates Bycium+ cell technology, and incorporates quality-enhancing and 
efficiency-increasing technologies such as SMBB and high-density packaging. The mass production efficiency reaches 22.5%, and the 
highest power output of the 72-cell module can reach 630W. Compared to P-type modules, it possesses lower BOS costs and advantages 
in LCOE. 
 
The company has established performance targets and implemented equity incentives. The company plans to grant 34million restricted 
shares and 80.21 million stock options to 2,733 individuals. The performance evaluation targets include achieving operating revenues of 
RMB 100/120/150 billion, or net profits of RMB 9.5/12.5/16 billion for the years 2023 to 2025. These targets will provide confidence in 
achieving growth in performance. 
 
Profit Forecast and Investment Recommendation: Based on the company's impressive performance in the first half of the year and the 
improved equity incentive mechanism, we have revised upwards the company's profit forecast for 2023-2025. We estimate that the 
company's net profit attributable to shareholders for 2023-2025 will be RMB 9.499/12.195/15.595 billion, respectively, with diluted EPS 
of RMB 2.87/3.68/4.71. As a leading company in N-type integrated capacity, we expect the company's business capabilities to continue 
to strengthen as domestic and overseas capacity is gradually released. Additionally, the company may enjoy a certain valuation premium 
compared to comparable companies. However, considering the downward shift in the valuation range of the sector and the 1.4-to-1 
stock split in late April, we assign a 2023 PE ratio of 18 times (previously 24 times) and lower the target price to RMB 51.66 (previously 
RMB 61.43), while maintaining an "Outperform" rating. 
 
Risks: lower-than-expected capacity utilization, intensified industry competition, fluctuations in raw material prices, etc.
