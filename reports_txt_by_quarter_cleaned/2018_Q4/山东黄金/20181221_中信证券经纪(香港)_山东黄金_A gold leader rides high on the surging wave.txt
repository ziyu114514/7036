Chief Non-ferrous
The Company is a leader in Chinas gold industry Its four major mines are
among the TOP10 gold mines in the country Asset injection from the
parent group is expected to support the rapid growth of the Companys
future gold production while acquisition of Barrick Gold Corporations
Veladero Gold Mine has kick started the Companys global expansion
strategy As the purest target in the domestic gold industry the Company
has seen a visible increase in its valuation during the gold price rising
cycle We give a 17x EVEBITDA valuation for the Company corresponding
to a reasonable market cap of Rmb841bn next year We raise the target
price to Rmb380 and retain our BUY rating
A leader with purest business in the gold industry enjoys a big upside for
valuation Gold business respectively accounts for 99% and 95 of the
Companys aggregate operating revenue and gross margin As the purest gold
target in China the Companys market performance is highly correlated with
gold price The Companys valuation level is expected to increase significantly
in the gold price rising cycle and the Companys future gold production growth
is highly deterministic which further supports its valuation level
Excellent resource endowment supports the Companys rapid growth in
gold production The four main mines of the Company are among the TOP10
gold mines in China with 850 tonnes in gross gold reserves and a gold output
of 2939 tonnes in 2017 ranking first in the country Their mining costs are
lower than the average in China All these four main mines have plans to
expand production in the future Meanwhile Shandong Gold Group a major
shareholder of the Company has remarkable reserve increases with gold
reserves as high as 1700 tonnes In 2017 its output of gold outside the listed
group the Companys gold production will see direct growth in the future with
its long-term output expected to reach 50 tonnes which will support the
Companys rapid earnings growth
Acquisition of Veladero Gold Mine kick starts global expansion The
Company acquired Barrick Gold Corporations Veladero Gold Mine in 2017
which increases its gold reserves to 987 tonnes Veladero Gold Mine is
expected to contribute more than 9 tonnes to the Companys gold output in
2018 The injection of mature gold assets supports the rapid growth of the
Companys gold production and earnings which will open up its strategic
expansion worldwide and offer a broad upside for future growth
Potential risks Gold price volatility and less-than-expected gold production
